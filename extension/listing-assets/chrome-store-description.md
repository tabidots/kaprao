# Kaprao Popup Dictionary for Thai

**Stop struggling and start reading real Thai content!** Hover over any Thai word on a webpage for instant definitions, romanization, and audio pronunciation. Kaprao intelligently segments Thai text into words for you—no more guessing where one word ends and another begins.

## Key features

### 📖 Smart Word Segmentation

Thai doesn't use spaces between words, making it challenging even for advanced learners to know where to break sentences. Kaprao uses sophisticated algorithms and a comprehensive dictionary to automatically find word boundaries—just hover and read!

### 🔤 Comprehensive Dictionary (140,000+ entries)

- Core dataset: Volubilis dictionary (~110,000 hand-curated entries)
- Supplemented with ~30,000 entries from Wiktionary (English & Thai)
- Rigorously cleaned for consistency and accuracy

### 🌍 Transliterations of Foreign Words (50,000+ entries)

An unprecedented dataset of foreign words, personal names, and place names mined from Thai Wikipedia—essential for reading news, social media, and real-world content.

### 🎨 Multiple Thai Fonts for Better Recognition

Thai word shapes change dramatically across fonts. Build stronger recognition skills by switching between:

- Loopless (modern, clean)
- Looped (traditional)
- Playful (casual)
- Handwritten (challenging but authentic)

### 🔊 Audio Pronunciation

Hear how words are pronounced with built-in text-to-speech support.

### ✨ Modified AUA-style Romanization

Clear, learner-friendly phonetic transcription using one of the most popular romanization systems for Thai.

### ⚡ Lightweight & Fast

Engineered for speed: Kaprao works seamlessly without slowing down your browser.

## How to Use

1. **Hover** over any Thai word to see the popup
2. Press **Alt+3** to hear pronunciation
3. Change fonts with **Alt+W** to practice recognition
4. Toggle the extension on/off with **Alt+K**

## Tips for Best Results

### 🔊 Better Thai Voice (Mac users)

Go to System Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → System Voice and select "Narisa (Enhanced)" or "Kanya (Enhanced)" for higher-quality Thai pronunciation.

### ⌨️ Customize Shortcuts

Visit [chrome://extensions/shortcuts] to change keyboard shortcuts to your preference.

### 📚 Perfect for Reading Practice

Use Kaprao on Thai news sites, blogs, social media, or any Thai content online. The more you read with instant lookup, the faster you'll build vocabulary!

**Works best on:** Blog posts, news articles, Wikipedia, and formal writing  
**May have limitations with:** Social media slang, informal abbreviations, and proper names (businesses, people, places) which may cause incorrect word splitting

## Romanization Guide (Modified AUA)

### Consonants

- Voiced: **b, d**
- Unaspirated: **p, t, k, j** (AUA: *c*)
- Aspirated: **ph, th, kh, ch**
- Other: **h, r, l, y, f, m, n, ng** (AUA: *ŋ*)

### Vowels

- Short: **a, i, u, e, o, ʉ, ə, ɔ, ɛ**
- Long: **aa, ii, uu, ee, oo, ʉʉ, əə, ɔɔ, ɛɛ**

**Diphthongs:** **ao, ia, ʉa, ua, iaw, iw, ɛw, ay, əy, ɔy, uay**
**Tones:** **a** (mid), **á** (high), **à** (low), **â** (falling), **ǎ** (rising)
